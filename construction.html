Frequently Asked Question

Our team consists of skilled professionals, from architects and engineers to project managers and craftsmen, all united by a common goal: to deliver exceptional results on time and within budget. We pride ourselves on our collaborative approach, working closely with clients to understand their vision and bring it to life.

At, Raktu Constructions Private, we understand that every project is more than just bricks and mortar; it's about creating spaces that inspire, innovate, and endure. From residential to commercial, from renovations to new constructions, we bring expertise and dedication to every job, ensuring unparalleled craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Before any project can begin, we undertake a comprehensive site analysis to better understand the given space, as well as the wider environment. We've labelled this framework as ‘design by research', an approach that is inspired by our academic links whilst qualified through practical experiences. At the same time, we adopt an interactive and collaborative approach, engaging closely and openly with clients (and their project team) to establish goals and ideas, and then put these into practice.
